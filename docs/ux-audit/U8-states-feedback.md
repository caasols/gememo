# U8 — States & feedback

## Summary

The extension has a solid foundation of state styling but suffers from **coverage gaps across element roles** and **semantic inconsistencies between surfaces**. Focus-visible coverage is the most critical gap: the majority of interactive controls (tabs, toggle, collapse buttons, rule-action buttons, log-group headers, snapshot header, close-overlay buttons, and all form inputs) have no `:focus-visible` ring at all, which is both an accessibility failure and a visual-consistency failure. Transition timing is fragmented across four distinct durations (0.1 s, 0.15 s, 0.2 s, 0.3 s) with no design rationale. The `.copied` success pattern exists for copy buttons but is applied inconsistently: `copy-action-items` (popup.js:919) never adds `.copied`, and the "Retrying…/Failed ✗/Sent ✓" pattern for retry buttons is a different ad-hoc scheme entirely. The overlay buttons in `content_meet.js` are fully inline-styled and share no token or state style with the popup system.

---

## Proposed standard (state matrix)

Every interactive role must define the states below. Transition timing should be unified to **two values only**: `0.1 s` for instant-feedback states (hover, active-press) and `0.15 s ease` for animated states (toggle slide, chevron rotate, copied fade).

| Role | Default | Hover | Focus-visible | Active (pressed) | Disabled | Success (.copied / done) | Loading / in-progress |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `.btn` (ghost) | border+text | `--hover-primary` bg, `--focus` border | `outline: 2px solid --focus; offset: 2px` | brightness(0.93) | `opacity: 0.4; cursor: not-allowed` | `.copied`: `--success-text` color, `--success` border | spinner or text change + `disabled` |
| `.btn-capture-now` (primary) | `--primary` bg | `--primary-dark` bg | `outline: 2px solid --focus; offset: 2px` | brightness(0.93) | `opacity: 0.6; cursor: not-allowed` (exists) | — | `disabled` + text "Capturing notes…" (exists) |
| `.tab` | muted text | `--text` color | `outline: 2px solid --focus; offset: -2px` | — | — | — | — |
| Toggle (`input[type=checkbox]` + track) | off-track | — | `outline: 2px solid --primary; offset: 2px` on track | — | `opacity: 0.4` on wrap | — | — |
| `.btn-collapse` | surface-subtle | `--hover-primary` bg (exists) | `outline: 2px solid --focus; offset: 2px` | — | — | — | — |
